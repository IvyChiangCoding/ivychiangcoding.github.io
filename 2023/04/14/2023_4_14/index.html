Words and Phrases for Trend Description in IELTS Task 1

Posted on 2023-04-14

[TOC]

Prompt

I asked ChatGPT to summarize useful words and phrases, and these words and phrases should be devided into 3 catagories: Words and Phrases for Number, such as ‘fall by 5%’ and ‘rise by 5%’; Words and Phrases for Vague Description, such as ‘the use of renwable energy has been increasing since 2000’; the last catagory is Others, which means you should add other excellent words and phrases that don’t belong to the 2 catagories above into this catagory.

The Summary

Here is its simple summary:

Words and Phrases for Number:

  • Increase by…
  • Decrease by…
  • Rise by…
  • Fall by…
  • Climb by…
  • Drop by…
  • Surge by…
  • Plummet by…
  • Double…
  • Triple…
  • Quadruple…

Words and Phrases for Vague Description:

  • There has been a steady increase in…
  • There has been a sharp decline in…
  • The trend has been upward/downward…
  • The use of… has been on the rise/fall…
  • There has been a significant growth/decline in…
  • Over the past few years/decades/centuries, there has been a gradual shift towards…
  • In recent times, there has been a noticeable change in…
  • A growing number of… are…

Others:

  • In line with…
  • In keeping with…
  • Consistent with…
  • As a result of…
  • Due to…
  • Owing to…
  • Thanks to…
  • On account of…
  • In light of…
  • With regard to…
  • Concerning…
  • Regarding…
  • In terms of…
  • As regards…
  • With respect to…
  • In connection with…
  • In reference to…
  • In relation to…

Examples for each expression

Then I ask it to give me simple examples for each expression, here is the answer:
Sure, here are some trend-related examples:

Words and Phrases for Number:

  • “The number of tourists visiting the city rose by 20% in the past year.”
  • “The amount of plastic waste generated by households fell by 15% over the past decade.”
  • “The percentage of people using public transportation climbed by 5% last month.”
  • “The rate of deforestation in the Amazon rainforest dropped by 25% in the past year.”
  • “The volume of e-commerce sales surged by 50% during the pandemic.”

Words and Phrases for Vague Description:

  • “There has been a steady increase in the adoption of electric vehicles since the 2010s.”
  • “There has been a sharp decline in the use of coal for electricity generation in recent years.”
  • “The trend has been upward for remote work, as more companies offer flexible work arrangements.”
  • “There has been a significant growth in the popularity of plant-based diets in the past few years.”
  • “Over the past decade, there has been a gradual shift towards renewable energy sources for electricity generation.”

Others:

  • “In line with the global trend, the country has set ambitious targets for reducing greenhouse gas emissions.”
  • “In keeping with our commitment to sustainability, we have implemented a recycling program in our office.”
  • “The company’s actions are consistent with the growing demand for eco-friendly products.”
  • “As a result of the pandemic, there has been a shift towards online learning and remote education.”
  • “Due to the increasing awareness of environmental issues, more consumers are choosing eco-friendly products.”
  • “Owing to advancements in technology, there has been a significant transformation in the healthcare industry.”
  • “Thanks to innovative solutions, we have been able to reduce our energy consumption and carbon footprint.”
  • “On account of changing consumer preferences, the food industry has been adapting to offer more plant-based options.”
  • “In light of the trend towards sustainable fashion, more brands are adopting eco-friendly practices.”
  • “With regard to the trend towards urbanization, cities are facing new challenges in terms of infrastructure and sustainability.”
  • “Concerning the growing importance of renewable energy, there is a need for investment in research and development.”
  • “Regarding the trend towards digitalization, businesses need to adapt and incorporate new technologies.”
  • “In terms of the trend towards automation, there are concerns about the impact on employment and job security.”
  • “As regards the trend towards remote work, there are opportunities for greater flexibility and work-life balance.”
  • “With respect to the trend towards conscious consumerism, there is a need for increased transparency and ethical practices in the fashion industry.”
  • “In connection with the trend towards smart cities, there is potential for improved efficiency and sustainability.”
  • “In reference to the trend towards circular economy, there is a need for innovative solutions to reduce waste and promote reuse and recycling.”
  • “In relation to the trend towards sustainable tourism, there is a need for responsible and eco-friendly practices in the travel industry.”

Examples in Ohthers don’t relate to trend description at all. But they’re good expressions which may be useful in other situstions, so I won’t delete them.
